Privacy policy

Last updated: March 2026

What we collect

  • Email address — to create and manage your account
  • Location data — to generate routes and track activities
  • Activity data — distance, pace, time and routes completed
  • Road reports — submitted voluntarily by you

How we use it

  • To provide the Routeio service
  • To generate personalised routes from your location
  • To save your activity history
  • We never sell your data to third parties

Third party services

  • Mapbox — for maps and routing (mapbox.com/legal/privacy)
  • Supabase — for secure data storage (supabase.com/privacy)
  • Strava — only if you choose to connect (strava.com/legal/privacy)
  • RevenueCat — for subscription management (revenuecat.com/privacy)

Your rights

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at ken@routeio.app. We will process all deletion requests within 30 days.

Contact

For any privacy related questions please email ken@routeio.app
