Beta

First beta release 🎉

The first version of Routeio is here. Generate routes, navigate them live, upload to Strava and track your activity history — all from one app.

New
  • Smart route generation — set your distance and activity, get a real route
  • Loop and point-to-point routing modes
  • Live GPS navigation with turn-by-turn directions
  • Pause and resume mid-activity
  • Post-activity summary with distance, pace and time
  • Strava integration — upload activities with one tap
  • GPX export for Garmin, Wahoo and other devices
  • Road issue reporting — share hazards with the community
  • Save and manage your favourite routes
  • Location search — plan routes from anywhere in the world
  • Running, cycling, trail running and hiking activity types
  • km and miles support
